Squat and accessories cycle

Home

This is a pretty intense squatting cycle. So if you have a decent training age with squatting this is a good place to start. What I would suggest for people that arent used to high volume and high intensity is to go through this initially at moderate weights. For males I would suggest the 1RM squat numbers be the equivalent of 75% to 100% of your body weight. For females the 1RM squat numbers should be 50% to 75% of bodyweight.

Rate of perceived exertion (RPE)

10 Maximal effort Feels impossible to keep going. No reps left in tank. 100% 1RM
9 Very hard activity Difficult to maintain at this level of exercise. Could do 1 more rep. About 96% 1RM
7 - 8 Vigorous Activity Close to becoming uncomfortable, can't speak while exercising. 2 - 3 reps left in tank. 90 - 92% 1RM
4 - 6 Moderate Activity Breathing heavily and can hold a conversation. 4 - 6 reps left in the tank. About 85% 1RM
2 - 3 Light Activity Feels like you can carry on for hours
1 Very light Activity Watching TV, driving a car etc.
